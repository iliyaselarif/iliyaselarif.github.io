About Us

Prestige Worldwide was founded in 2020 by a small group of computer science students who saw an opportunity to change how we as a society can learn about managing our money. After none of us were affected by a stock market crash, but reading about how difficult it probably was, we didn't want to see anyone suffer like that. By creating this website we are working towards a more prosperous future.
